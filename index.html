We say goodbye.

Hello Meet has been discontinued

After 7 years of operation, we have discontinued Hello Meet in 2024.

Unfortunately, we dont have the time or funds to continue the app. We would have liked to continue to maintain and expand the app - for example, with scheduling by time.

It pains us to discontinue Hello Meet. But the climate crisis requires that we devote more of our time and attention to addressing it. May we succeed so that we can revive Hello Meet later.

If you would like Hello Meet to continue, get in touch.

Your Hello Meet Team
